Curiosity of the Week

WWII Silver Thruppence "Three Reigns" Sweetheart Bracelet

British soldiers who were stationed overseas during WWII used to make these "sweetheart bracelets" to send to their significant others back home. This particular example is made from nine .925 silver 3-pence coins, dated 1882, 1889, 1898, 1902, 1912, 1913, 1936, 1940 and 1941.
